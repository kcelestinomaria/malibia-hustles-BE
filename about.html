Who We Are

& why we exist

Malibia Business Engineering is Kenya's first, and arguably the world's first Business Engineering platform.
Founded in late 2020, The whole idea behind the platform was to help Kenyans easily start and manage hustles. Consequently, our partners get to enjoy passive income from their hustles as well as constant technological, business-wise and legal support for their hustles. All of which do not require upfront payments of any kind.

The Problem

Malibia was a response to the increased job layoffs and income reduction that came about as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic effects on not only the Kenyan economy, but also the World economy.
We evaluated the worrying scenario of decreased income and wealth creation in Kenya.

According to a Kenya National Bureau of Statistics report published in late 2020 by Business Daily Africa, nearly half of Kenyans earn below 30,000 Kenya shillings, which means that a big number of Kenyan households are burdened in acquiring basic needs. This shows that Kenya's economic growth has failed to reciprocate in terms of income growth. This problem has even worsened under the Covid-19 pandemic which led to job losses and salary cuts.

Our Solution


Malibia Business Engineering aims to solve this problem of income inequality by giving Kenyans the opportunity to create hustles when they need to and with free professional support.
Our services ensure that anyone anywhere in Kenya can create a hustle and still do other things while his/her hustle is managed effectively by our technology infrastructure and business savoir-faire.
