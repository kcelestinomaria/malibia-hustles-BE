Who We Are

Get to know more about our company

Malibia Business Engineering is Kenya's first, and arguably the world's first Business Engineering platform. Founded in late 2020, The whole idea behind the platform was to help Kenyans easily start and manage hustles.
Malibia was a response to the increased job layoffs and income reduction that came about as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic effects on not only the Kenyan economy, but also the World economy.
Malibia Engineering employs software as well as emerging technology to automate the business processes of hustles, thereby automating them. We also take away all the stress that comes with legal and regulatory hurdles, and we help hustles secure equity for growth through our venture partners
