Business Contracts Management & Enforcing

Business contracts are very essential in the success of the business or a hustle in this particular case itself as it enables getting projects and tasks done. They also reinforce trust in business partnerships which ameliorates a party's ease of doing business.

At Malibia, we ensure that your hustle acquires respectable contracts in its line of partnering with other business entities and customers. Currently, we are building a smart contracts system on the Blockstacks Blockchain ecosystem using Clarity Smart Contracts This will ensure we automate the processing of contracts which speeds up the making and enforcing of contracts when our partner hustles do business deals with us and with other business parties

Mobile, Web & Emerging Technology Integrations

We also infuse mobile, web and emerging technology integrations into your hustle. This automates the business processes of your hustle such as the hiring process(if needed), the revenue accruals and adaptability scenarios. We also use Artificial Intelligence to optimize you hustle as well as be able to collect, analyze and make meaningful all the data that is generated from the daily operations of your hustle. The latter will help you in making decisions on how and where you want to lead your hustle

You will also be able to monitor the progress and daily operations of your hustle from a Progressive Web Application on your phone(Android or iPhone) and your laptop
Lastly, we are working on developing a smart contracts ecosystem to automate the process of making business contracts in your hustles so that you can make business partnerships seamlessly and quickly to grow your hustle as fast as you want.
